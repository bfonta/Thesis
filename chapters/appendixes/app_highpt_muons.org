In [[tab:highPt_fractions]] we present the fractions of \mutau{} events with $\pt(\mu)>120\,\si{\GeV}$.
We include all radion signal samples and the two most important backgrounds, \ac{DY} and $\ttbar$.
The fraction is small for most samples, and its maximum value is \SI{\sim 20}{\percent}.
