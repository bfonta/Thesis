The search reported in this thesis makes use of the /modified frequentist approach/, a statistical method originally developed for Higgs boson searches at \ac{LEP} [[cite:&cls1]].
Since then, it has been extensively used by searches and combinations from the \ac{LHC} experiments, notably for the combination paper reporting the observation of the Higgs [[cite:&asimov;&stat_procedure_comb_higgs]].
